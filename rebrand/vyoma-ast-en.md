## **VYOMA – Advanced Security Testing & Risk Assessment with Vulnerability Analysis**

### **1. Name Meaning**

**VYOMA (व्योम)** means *sky, vast space, boundless domain*.
In the context of cybersecurity, "Vyoma" represents the extensive, deep, and comprehensive scope of analysis — as vast as the sky.

Risk evaluation based on:

### **4. Technical Acronym**

**VYOMA = Verified Yield of Offensive & Mitigation Assessments**

### **5. Brand Narrative**

**VYOMA** is a platform designed for organizations that require advanced security testing and accurate risk assessment. With the philosophy of "boundless space," VYOMA combines manual analysis, automation, and intelligence-driven assessment to uncover complex vulnerabilities that are often undetected by conventional tools.

VYOMA maps risks with precision, prioritizes mitigation, and provides strategic recommendations that are measurable, helping organizations achieve a strong, adaptive security posture ready to face modern threats.
